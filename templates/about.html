About EXCEL-K

EXCEL-K is dedicated to providing information about respiratory health, particularly focusing on lung diseases such as pulmonary disease. Pulmonary disease encompasses a range of conditions affecting the lungs, from chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) to asthma and lung cancer. Our mission is to educate individuals about the prevention, management, and treatment options available for these conditions.
