Lung Health Questions

  • How can I prevent lung diseases? - Here are some tips to maintain lung health:
    • Maintain a healthy diet with foods rich in antioxidants and vitamins.
    • Avoid smoking and exposure to secondhand smoke.
    • Exercise regularly to improve lung function.
    • Stay up to date with vaccinations, such as flu and pneumonia vaccines.
    • Avoid exposure to pollutants and chemicals that can harm lung tissue.
  • What are common symptoms of lung diseases? - Symptoms may include coughing, shortness of breath, chest pain, and fatigue. Consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
