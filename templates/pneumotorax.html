Various Examples of Pneumothoraks

Pneumothorax is a condition where air enters the pleural space, the space between the lungs and the chest wall, causing the lung to collapse.

Prevention Tips
  • Avoid activities that may cause chest injury, such as extreme sports without protection.
  • If you have a history of pneumothorax, consult a doctor for proper management.
  • Watch out for symptoms such as sudden chest pain or shortness of breath.
